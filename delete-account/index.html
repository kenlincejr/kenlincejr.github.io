Account deletion

Delete your PilotBriefAI account & data

PilotBriefAI provides an in-app account deletion flow for permanently removing your account and associated data.

Primary method: in-app deletion

Open PilotBriefAI → Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your email address, personal minimums, and all briefing and error records associated with your account.

Alternate method: web request

If you can't access the app, email ken.lince@gmail.com from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my account." We will permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Email address and account identity.
  • Personal minimums and preferences.
  • Saved routes and favorite airports.
  • Aircraft and student profiles.
  • Briefing and error records linked to the account.

What may be retained

Anonymized, non-identifying service telemetry already disassociated from your account may persist up to 90 days for operational monitoring. Subscription records held by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat are governed by those platforms.

For more detail, see the Privacy Policy.
