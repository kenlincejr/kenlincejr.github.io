Pilot support

Need help with PilotBriefAI?

Open PilotBriefAI → Settings → Support to file a bug, ask a question, or open a support request from inside the app.

Primary support channel: PilotBriefAI includes support requests directly inside the Settings tab. This lets us receive the right account and app context with your message.

Response expectation

We typically review in-app support requests within 1-2 business days. CFI subscribers receive priority support.

Before every flight

PilotBriefAI is a situational-awareness aid, not an official weather briefing. Always verify critical flight information through FAA-approved sources before flight.

FAQ

Is PilotBriefAI an official weather briefing?

No. PilotBriefAI is a situational-awareness aid. It does not replace an official preflight briefing from an FAA-approved source such as 1800wxbrief.com or Flight Service.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Every authenticated pilot gets full IFR-tier access for 14 days with no credit card required. The trial is limited to one per account.

How do I cancel?

Cancel through your device's App Store or Google Play subscription settings. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

Open PilotBriefAI → Settings → Account → Delete Account, or review the public deletion instructions at avi8ai.com/delete-account/.
