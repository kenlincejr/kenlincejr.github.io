Privacy Policy — PilotBriefAI

PilotBriefAI ("we", "us", or "our") is an aviation situational-awareness application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address and a password hash (stored by Supabase Auth).
  • Location. With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to identify your nearest airport and display your aircraft's position on sectional charts. Location is processed on-device and is not stored on our servers.
  • Personal minimums. Weather limits and preferences you enter (e.g. ceiling minimums, crosswind limits) are stored in your account so they persist across devices.
  • Briefing telemetry. We log anonymized metadata for each briefing request: departure and destination ICAO codes, go/no-go result, response latency, and AI token counts. Used to monitor service health; not linked to you by name.
  • Error logs. Anonymized crash and error reports that help us diagnose bugs.
  • Subscription information. Purchases are handled by RevenueCat; we receive subscription status but not payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app.
  • Authenticate you and sync your personal minimums across devices.
  • Generate AI-assisted preflight briefings based on the airports you enter.
  • Monitor usage patterns and service reliability (anonymized).
  • Communicate service updates or account notices via email.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and edge-function hosting.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI model used to generate briefing summaries. Briefing content is sent to Anthropic's API and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
  • RevenueCat — in-app subscription management.
  • FAA / NOAA / aviationweather.gov — publicly available aviation weather and NOTAM data fetched on your behalf.
  • LiveATC.net — publicly streamed ATC audio.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. Anonymized briefing telemetry and error logs are retained for up to 90 days for operational monitoring.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account inside the app. Upon deletion we permanently remove your email address, personal minimums, and all briefing and error records associated with your account.

6. Children

PilotBriefAI is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
